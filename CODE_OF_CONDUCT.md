# Code of Conduct

GBIF Workbench follows a simple standard: be respectful, evidence-driven, and useful.

Contributors should:

- discuss ideas and code on their merits,
- assume good intent while still being precise about problems,
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language,
- respect privacy and do not share private analyses, credentials, or personal data.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that make the project unsafe or unproductive.
